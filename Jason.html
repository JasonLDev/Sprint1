Italian Food

Italy has a variety of dishes that can sate almost any type of appetite. From pastas with sauces and meats to deserts with a strong enough coffee flavor to keep even the most sugar hungry people satisfied!

Pizza

Pizza is probably one of, if not, the most popular dishes in Italian cuisine. Pizza can be made with a variety of toppings for different flavors. Generally it is made with a large cicrle shaped dough with tomato sauce on top and cheese after the sauce. It would be put into either a wood oven or electric oven to make the dough rise and the cheese melt. After a certain amount of time depending on the oven, the pizza will be cooked and ready to eat!

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a Italian desert that a very deep coffee flavor and has been popular for as long as 1960! This desert is generally made from ladyfinger pastries (savoiardi) dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone, and flavoured with cocoa.

Risotto

Risotto is a rice based dish that is cooked with broth in a way that will eventually turn the dish into a creamy texture. The broth that is used for the risotto can contain a variety of things such as seafood, meat, or vegetable . Many types of risotto contain butter, onion, white wine, and Parmesan cheese..

Lasagna

Lasagna is another very well known Italian dish and is a staple for a really filling dinner for a large amount of people. lasagna is one of the oldest types of pasta.
