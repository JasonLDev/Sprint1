Spain's dishes

Spain is known for its choice of seafood & shellfish in many of its dishes, along with seasonings such as Saffron that has made it all the way into the caribbean through Spanish colonization

Gazpacho

Commonly eaten in andalucia. A blend of tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, bread, peppers and cucumbers, served chilled.

Paella

a dish of saffron-flavoured rice cooked with meats, seafood, and vegetables. Originating in the rice-growing areas on Spain's Mediterranean coast, the dish is especially associated with the region of Valencia.

Patatas Bravas

a traditional Spanish tapas dish consisting of crispy, fried potatoes drizzled in a smoky salsa brava made with tomatoes and smoked paprika

Spanish tortilla

Spanish tortilla is a traditional dish from Spain. It is celebrated as one of the most popular dishes of the Spanish cuisine. It is an omelette made with eggs and potatoes, usually including onion. It is often served at room temperature as a tapa
